## recipe-io (should be read as "recipio")

The ultimate food recipe interface to make your life easier. You load your recipes and it shows the instructions but also if you select more than one recipe you generate a global list of all the ingredients needed for all the recipes. 

### recipe-io

Recipe-io is a cutting edge code that runs locally on your phone or PC. Just donwload and open a browser

- recipe.html
- recipe.json
